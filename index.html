Welcome to Sheidow.org

Sheidow.org is a hub for my explorations in web development, cybersecurity, and tech innovation. Hosted on a Raspberry Pi, this site is a testament to the power of DIY technology and the endless possibilities that come with learning and building.

Areas of Focus

Automation

Dive into projects that leverage automation to streamline everyday tasks, whether it's managing a smart home or optimizing workflows. Explore how technology can do the heavy lifting.

Customization

Discover the art of customization through tailored scripts, personalized web servers, and open-source tools. Whether for work or play, find out how to make technology work for you.

Cybersecurity

Security isn't just a feature; it's a priority. Follow along as I delve into the latest in cybersecurity practices, apply them to my projects, and share insights on keeping systems safe.
